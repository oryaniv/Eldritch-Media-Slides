Eldritch Media Slides

is an easy-to-use, feature packed javascript library that enables you, be you a seasoned developer or even no-programmer, to create beautiful slideshows and easily embed them in your webpage. Eldritch Media Slides is compatible with all up-to-date browsers and works neatly on most platforms.

key features including:

  • Animated image transitions
  • color filtering on transitions
  • Customizable texts on every slide, text,colors,fonts, etc
  • Animated texts
  • Slideshows with audio
  • Control bar on slideshow
  • customizable slideshow size
  • a rich programmer API
  • And more!

so, what are you waiting for? you can have your amazing slideshow right away!
